Learn a bit about me

I took sometime to figure out what I wanted to or where I exactly wanted to be.
I knew I loved working with people and also being able to create weither it be on a device or using paper.
Once I learned more about Web Design in a class I knew this would be my passion. I tend to play it safe with colors but when there is a chance I love to explore colors on sites! My design theory has been about trying to make a site feel alive. Looking for adventure, peace or somewhere to travel? A site should provide you with all the information but also take you on a journey straight from a mobile device.
